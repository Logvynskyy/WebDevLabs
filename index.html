    BMW M5

    Some information about

    The BMW M5 is a high performance variant of the BMW 5 Series marketed under the BMW M sub-brand.

    It is considered an iconic vehicle in the sports sedan category.

    The first M5 model was hand-built in 1985 on the E28 535i chassis with a modified engine from the M1 that made it the fastest production sedan at the time.

    Versions:

    • E28 M5 (1984–1988);
    • E34 M5 (1988–1995);
    • E39 M5 (1998–2003);
    • E60/E61 M5 (2004–2010);
    • F10 M5 (2011–2016);
    • F90 M5 (2017–present).
